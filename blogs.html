New (and first) Blog!

This first post is more of an overview than anything. You will also find this information in the "About" page.

Welcome to stormbreaker's blog! As of right now, this is not a regularly updated blog nor do I expect it to ever be. However, whenever there is a new update to any of my projects (when I have the chance to work on any projects), you will be sure to find information about it here. I may also post links to and discussions about advances in the technological/programming world. You may also find the occasional rant about issues not related to technology, but rather my thoughts on society. In addition, if I have some pretty crazy ideas for technology I may post them here.

Yes, I know my whole site and the blog posts in particular are a bit "plain" right now. I threw this current version up in about a week and a half with little to no experience in either HTML or CSS. My personal thoughts are that this is good (not excellent but good) for a first attempt with a limited amount of time. I expect to be able to revise, adapt, expand, and otherwise improve the site bit by tiny bit over the next few months. Please bear with me and enjoy!
